About Leon

Founder | Engineer | Researcher

I'm a founder and engineer who has worked across research labs, startups, and Fortune 500 clients. I build whatever needs building.


At MIT Media Lab I developed gesture recognition systems for NASA's Astrobee robot, designing hand gestures to help deaf astronauts communicate in space. I'm currently collaborating with UCF's ISUE Lab under Dr. Joseph LaViola, leading research on situational awareness for VR displays.


I've shipped production code for Google Design and Microsoft Unlocked through Instrument, built health tech infrastructure at Mindscape Health, and created accessibility tools that actually get used.


Currently building at Atano in Miami. Originally from Kenya. 15+ hackathon wins including at MIT, Stanford, and Harvard. GitHub Campus Expert.


I care about technology that works under real constraints and doesn't leave people out.
